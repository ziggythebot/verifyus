# VerifyUS Landing Page Copy (Optimized)

## Hero Section

### Headline Options

**Option A (Recruiter-focused):**
```
Your recruiting team is drowning in bot applications.
```

**Rationale:** Direct present pain. Triggers Present Bias + Jobs to Be Done. "Drowning" = vivid, emotional, relatable.

**Option B (Combined pain):**
```
Stop Wasting 10+ Hours Per Role on Fake Applicants.
```

**Rationale:** Specific, quantified pain. Terminal-style caps fit aesthetic. Leads with time (immediate), sets up money loss later.

**Option C (Question format):**
```
How Much Time Did Your Team Waste on Bot Applications This Week?
```

**Rationale:** Makes them calculate their own pain. Activates Availability Heuristic (recent bot spam).

---

**RECOMMENDED: Option A**
Most visceral, terminal aesthetic, sets up contrast effect.

---

### Subheadline

```
VerifyUS blocks bot spam before it hits your ATS — saving your team 10+ hours per role and preventing the $50K+ fraudulent hires that sneak through.
```
